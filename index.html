Do Your chores First!

Bob just wants the Wi-Fi. But when he shows up at Grandma's cozy house, phone in hand, things don't go as planned. What starts with a simple ask turns into something else entirely. She's sweet. She's sharp. And she's not giving up that password easily. An interactive comic about effort, expectations — and one grandma who plays by her own rules.

Designers & Developers: Afra, Iqra Bano, Meera

About

subtitle

DoYourChoresFirst! is a short comic about a phone-obsessed teen, a crafty grandma, and one tricky Wi-Fi password. When Bob visits his cookie-baking grandmother, all he wants is internet access — but she has other plans.

After a full day of chores, Bob learns the password was Doyourchoresfirst all along. A funny, feel-good reminder to never underestimate Grandma.
